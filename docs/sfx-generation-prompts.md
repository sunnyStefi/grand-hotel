# Grand Hotel Gold — Sound Effects Generation Prompts

Generate each as a **short, punchy sound effect** (1–3 seconds). Use Suno or similar tool with these prompts.

---

## 1. Door Open — "ding"

**Prompt:**
A bright, cheerful **ding** sound — like a hotel bell or door chime. Single crisp note, 8-bit synth, pure and immediate. Should feel celebratory and instant (no sustain). ~300ms duration. Perfect for signaling a correct answer.

**Vibe:** Classic arcade + service bell + instant gratification.

---

## 2. Coin Collect — "ching"

**Prompt:**
A sparkling, cascading **ching** sound — like coins tumbling into a jar. Use bright, high-pitched synth arpeggios (xylophone-like or chiptune bells). Quick burst ~400ms, with a subtle trailing sparkle. Repeatable without fatigue (he'll hear this 100+ times).

**Vibe:** Satisfying treasure, nostalgic arcade coin-drop, pure joy.

---

## 3. Wrong Answer — "buzz"

**Prompt:**
A soft, non-punishing **buzz** or "nope" sound. Short raspberry/buzzer tone (~200ms), low-mid synth, gentle and quick. Should signal "try again" not "you failed." Forgiving, never harsh or sad.

**Vibe:** Gentle nudge, instant reset, no shame.

---

## 4. Combo Build — "combo"

**Prompt:**
A rising, ascending **arpeggio** sound — like a coin-counter ticking up or a power-up building. Bright 8-bit synth, quick ascending notes (C-E-G or similar), ~300ms total. Plays every time combo multiplier increases. Short enough to stack without clutter.

**Vibe:** Building momentum, rising excitement, chiptune magic.

---

## 5. Floor Clear / Fanfare — "fanfare"

**Prompt:**
A triumphant, celebratory **fanfare** — short arcade-style victory jingle. Bright, major-key synth melody (~1–2 seconds), hints of triumph or celebration. Punchy drums, maybe a cymbal swell. Should feel like winning, not overwhelming. Room for a follow-up cash-register **cha-ching** sound on the Build screen.

**Vibe:** Victory lap, badge of honor, "level complete!"

---

## Playback Notes

- All SFX are **loopable and stackable** — two "ching"s in rapid succession should sound natural, not jarring.
- **No silence between repeats** — each effect should end clean, ready for the next play.
- **Bright, warm palette** — match the gold/red/teal color scheme of the game.
- **Mono or stereo OK** — no surround or immersive audio needed.
- **Format:** MP3 or WAV, 44.1 kHz or higher.

---

## Build Screen SFX (Optional, if time allows)

**Cash register — "cha-ching"**
A classic cash-register *cha-ching* sound when he spends coins on rooms/floors. Satisfying, retro, ~500ms. Makes spending feel rewarding, not like a loss.
